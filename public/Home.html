Bukola Ayodele

Software Engineer & Content Creator

Bukola Ayodele is a Software Engineer and Content Creator based in NYC. Using her YouTube channel, Bukola shares her experience as a Black woman in the computer science fields and provides tips and advice for those interested in tech.

Shivali Kadam

Process Engineer

Shivali Kadam is a process engineer and Stem advocate living in Portland, OR. Born in Mumbai, India, she immigrated to the United States at the age of two with her parents. As a female engineer, she is passionate about increasing youth access to STEM outreach programming and serves on the Outreach & Education Committee of Women in Science PDX. She is currently serving as Miss Oregon and using her title to promote her social impact initiative, STEMpowered: Inspiring the Next Generation of Female Engineers and Scientists.

#STEMAdvocate #Professional

Shannon Li

UX/UI Engineer & Software Engineer

Shannon Li is working towards a career in UX and data science. She identifies as a design activist who aims to make design and technology inclusive. As a queer woman of color, Shannon recognizes the importance of intersectionality and advocating for social justice through her work.
